Introduction

My name is Julius Reade.

I created Masturbate Less in 2019 to help those struggling with their masturbation habits.

Masturbate Less is actually the sister website to NeverFap Deluxe, a website I created to help those suffering from porn addiction.

So if you feel as if you're actually addicted to porn rather than have a problem with masturbation, then I would recommend checking it out!

Motivation

As part of NeverFap Deluxe, I managed to speak with a lot of people in regards to their own personal problems with porn addiction.

And as it turns out, there were quite a lot of people out there who actually struggling with mastrubation and who didn't watch porn at all.

Which surprised me, since personally speaking the only reason why I masturbated was largely because I was addicted to porn.

So I thought I'd create this website to help those people who struggle solely with masturbation.

Thankfully a lot of the same principles apply, especially in regards to developing control over your mind.

If you would like to get in touch, please provide your name, email and message, and I will get back to you as soon as possible!

I also offer Personal Coaching for those interested in receiving additional help from someone who's been there before.
